What is Chemistry?

Chemistry is the branch of science concerned with the substances of which matter is composed, the investigation of their properties and reactions, and the use of such reactions to form new substances.

Why is Chemistry Important?

Chemistry deals with our surrounding. Everthing we consume in day to day life is related to chemistry in some way. The food we consume, the house we live in and the air we breathe all can be studied under chemistry.
